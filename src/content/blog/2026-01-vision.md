# Why CaptureEase?

## The Problem
Parenting is hard. Remembering every little detail about your child's day—moods, behaviors, medications—is impossible.

## The Solution
CaptureEase simplifies the chaos. We provide a seamless way to log, track, and analyze your child's daily journey.

## What's New in 2026?
- **Smart Classification**: Just type "He had a meltdown" and we tag it for you.
- **Enhanced Timeline**: See your child's day at a glance.
- **Secure Integration**: Your data is safe and easy to share with care teams.
